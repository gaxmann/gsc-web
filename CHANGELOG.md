## Project history

Have a look at the apk project page: https://github.com/gaxmann/gordonssunclock

If you are on iOS: The app is currently only available for Android. But this webpage should work with all devices that have current browsers. For a wall clock maybe the best option is still to buy a used Android tablet and hang it on the wall together with a 3 meter long USB cable and a timer that switches the charger on for about 2 hours in the morning and in the evening.
